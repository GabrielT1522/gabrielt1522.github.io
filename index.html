I am a Computer Engineering student at Texas A&M International University (TAMIU) with a passion for problem-solving and learning. I am proficient in programming languages such as C/C++, Python, and Java, and have experience in data management and web development.

I have also completed several bootcamps, including an Applied Research Bootcamp at TAMIU and the GET PHIT Bootcamp with UT Health Houston. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an active member of several professional societies, including the National Association of Rocketry, TAMIU Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS), and the TAMIU Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE). I am also a dedicated community member, serving as the Worship & Multimedia Director at Renacer Iglesia Cristiana and as a Percussion Technician at Martin High School.
